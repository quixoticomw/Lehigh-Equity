Introduction to CompSci


My first exposure came from when I had joined my first computer science class completely by accident. It wasn’t the most up-to-date, but I was handed a book on HTML5 and CSS3 from 2011. It gave me a taste for what coding was like and I learned that I wanted to continue practicing it. Even if an introduction isn’t ‘high quality’, it still provides exposure to something new!



For anyone else that may want to learn, a great free resource is freecodecamp.org! It’s a great resource to start out with as it’s segmented into small lessons!
